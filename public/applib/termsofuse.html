Access to the portal

Access to OpenEBench-VRE and the applications hosted is free and unrestricted. Upon first access, a unique URL is generated to allow recovering of the data space in later sessions.

 

Users desiring a longer interaction with Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox can register to obtain a permanent workspace.

Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox portal supports https access, which ensures encryption of all transferred data. Authentication protocols are applied transparently to all data transactions between the user and the Portal, either interactively, or through REST based APIs to assure privacy.

Users’ data

Our Service allows users to upload, and store information, research data, or other material, perform analysis on it, and download results. Additionally, Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox allows to access selected public data repositories.

 

User’s data and results obtained (since they are generated automatically without human intervention) are treated as confidential and remain the intellectual property of the user.

 

User’s data is held in a private virtual workspace. Unless agreed in other terms, users’ workspace is limited to 100 MB storage size, and is maintained until termination of users’ relationship with Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox. For unregistered users, data is held 90 days after creation. For registered users, in case of long term inactivity (6 months), Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox reserves the right to archive users’ data and release the used storage.

 

Data held in the workspace is only accessible by their owner, although admin operations staff may be requested to access it to solve eventual operational issues. When necessary, a confidentiality agreement may be formalized. It is the responsibility of the user to ensure that the required agreement is in place prior to uploading any confidential data.

 

Backup of users’ data is performed periodically following the internal policies of the hosting institution. Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox and/or the hosting institution cannot be held liable for data loss in users’ workspace. Users are advised to keep a local copy of any data uploaded and/or produced by Exposome Data Analysis Toolbox.
